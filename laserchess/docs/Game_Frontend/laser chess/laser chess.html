Piece guide

interactive help cards with sandbox-style previews

KING

The king is the target. If a laser destroys it, the game ends immediately.

SWITCH

Switches reflect from every side and can swap with defenders or deflectors.

DEFENDER

Blocks the beam from one side and is removed by hits from the others.

DEFLECTOR

Reflects the beam at right angles from one mirrored edge.

LASER

The emitter cannot move. Rotate it, then it fires across the board.
