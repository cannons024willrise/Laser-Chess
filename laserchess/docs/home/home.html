THE RULES

Tactical Operation Guide

01 THE PIECES

KING

TARGET

The main target. No mirrors, no shields. If any laser beam hits any of its four sides, that King is removed and the game is over instantly[cite: 3].

VULNERABILITY: 100%[cite: 3]

SWITCH

UTILITY

Mirrored on both sides. It cannot be eliminated. Special: Can swap positions with an adjacent Defender or Deflector without rotating[cite: 3].

STATUS: INVULNERABLE[cite: 3]

DEFENDER

SHIELD

Equipped with a shielded front that safely blocks lasers. It remains in play if hit on the shield, but is destroyed if hit on the sides or back[cite: 3].

VULNERABILITY: SIDE/REAR[cite: 3]

DEFLECTOR

MIRROR

The main tactical unit. One side reflects the beam at exactly 90°. If struck on any of the other three non-mirrored sides, it is eliminated[cite: 3].

VULNERABILITY: 3 SIDES[cite: 3]

LASER

WEAPON

Permanent beam source located in the corner. It cannot move or be removed. It can be rotated to fire down the first column or across the first row[cite: 3].

STATUS: PERMANENT[cite: 3]

02 YOUR TURN

Perform one of three actions[cite: 3]:

MOVE

Shift any piece one square in any direction (including diagonals). Orientation remains the same[cite: 3].

ROTATE

Turn any piece 90° clockwise or counter-clockwise on its current square[cite: 3].

SWAP

The Switch may change places with an adjacent Defender or Deflector of either color[cite: 3].

03 SYSTEM LOCKS

!

Blue pieces cannot enter squares marked with Red Helix symbols. Red pieces cannot enter squares marked with Blue Helix symbols[cite: 3].

!

No test firing. Once you remove your hand from a piece, the move is locked permanently and the laser must be fired[cite: 3].

04 // AUTO FIRE SEQUENCE

After every turn, your laser automatically fires[cite: 3]. The beam bounces off mirrors until it leaves the board or hits a piece[cite: 3]. Any piece struck on a non-mirrored side is instantly removed[cite: 3]. You may rotate your Laser before firing[cite: 3].FRIENDLY FIRE COUNTS. WATCH YOUR OWN BEAM[cite: 3].
